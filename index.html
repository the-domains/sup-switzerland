We're back!

After an exciting couple of years building our inflatable paddle board brand Origin Paddleboards in the UK (www.originpaddleboards.com), we are proud to announce we are back in Switzerland! In March 2016 we will be opening our very first swiss manufacturing facility in Interlaken, Berner Oberland. 

Lake Brienz

A 2 minute walk from our manufacturing facility is the bluest lake in Switzerland: Lake Brienz. Product testing, SUP rental and tours are all offered by SUP Switzerland throughout the summer months. Come and have a visit and be amazed by the scenery.
